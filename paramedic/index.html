Welcome to paramedic

Your health matters to us

A paramedic application that tries to shorten the response time for critical cases, especially those with chronic diseases, based on the involvement of qualified community groups in aiding patients, determining their whereabouts, and notifying them of the patient's whereabouts. of emergency situations.
This application also allows you to add your health history and medications, which makes it easier for paramedics to deal with you in a healthy manner appropriate to your health condition.

The individual downloads this application, then adds his data and sets up his own account within the application. In the event of an emergency, the user presses the “Service Request” button
